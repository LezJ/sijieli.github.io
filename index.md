## About Me

I am currently doing my Ph.D. at the University of Sheffield (UoS), working within the Computer Vision Research Group, advised by Prof. [Jungong Han](https://scholar.google.co.uk/citations?user=hNi1gxAAAAAJ&hl=en) and Dr. [Chen Chen](https://cherise215.github.io/). Previous to my PhD study, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) and my master's degree from University of Edinburgh (UoE), respectively. I have also served as a Machine Learning Engineer in industry for over two years. 

In research, I value intellectual depth and elegant simplicity.
